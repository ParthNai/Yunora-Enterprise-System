====================================================
YUNORA BUSINESS ERP, CRM & AUTOMATION SYSTEM
============================================

This Admin Panel must not be limited to managing products and orders.

It must function as a complete business management system.

====================================================
CRM (CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT)
======================================

Admin can:

• View Customer Journey
• Track Customer Activity
• Track Product Interests
• Track Cart Abandonment
• Track Purchase History

Customer Timeline:

• Registration Date
• Orders
• Warranty Claims
• Reviews
• Support Requests

====================================================
LEAD MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
======================

Capture leads from:

• Contact Form
• Dealer Form
• WhatsApp Inquiry
• Call Request
• Product Inquiry

Lead Status:

• New
• Contacted
• Interested
• Follow Up
• Converted
• Closed

====================================================
MARKETING AUTOMATION
====================

Admin can create:

• Email Campaigns
• WhatsApp Campaigns
• Festival Campaigns
• New Product Launch Campaigns

Schedule campaigns automatically.

====================================================
ABANDONED CART RECOVERY
=======================

If customer leaves cart:

Send:

• Email Reminder
• WhatsApp Reminder

Admin can track recovery rate.

====================================================
DELIVERY MANAGEMENT
===================

Integrate:

• Shiprocket
• Delhivery
• DTDC
• India Post

Admin can:

• Generate Shipping Labels
• Track Shipments
• Manage Delivery Status

====================================================
GST & BILLING SYSTEM
====================

Generate:

• GST Invoices
• Tax Reports
• Sales Reports

Support:

• GST Number
• HSN Codes
• Tax Breakdown

====================================================
SUPPORT TICKET SYSTEM
=====================

Customers can create:

• Product Support Tickets
• Warranty Tickets
• Delivery Tickets

Admin can:

• Reply
• Assign Staff
• Resolve Tickets

====================================================
BACKUP & RESTORE SYSTEM
=======================

Automatic Backups:

• Daily
• Weekly
• Monthly

One-click Restore.

====================================================
SYSTEM HEALTH MONITOR
=====================

Admin Dashboard shows:

• Server Health
• Database Health
• API Health
• Storage Usage
• Traffic Status

SUPER ADMIN REQUIREMENT
